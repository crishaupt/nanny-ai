Workflow Capabilities for Supply Chain & Distribution

Automate repetitive tasks while maintaining complete human oversight

Smart Automations

  • Pattern Detection: AI identifies repetitive tasks like daily inventory checks or weekly carrier reporting
  • Automation Proposals: Get step-by-step workflows explained in plain language before enabling
  • Approval Prompts: Set when you want to approve: every time, daily summary, or weekly review
  • Exception Handling: AI requests your input when situations don't match normal patterns
  • You Control Runtime: Decide when automations run and pause them anytime

System Updates

  • Order Management: Auto-update order status, shipping info, and delivery confirmations from carrier feeds
  • Inventory Systems: Propose stock level adjustments based on received shipments and cycle counts
  • Customer Records: Extract delivery preferences, address changes, and contact updates from emails
  • Supplier Data: Update lead times, minimum order quantities, and pricing from supplier notifications
  • No Auto-Commits: All database changes require your review and approval

Adaptive Guidance

  • Dynamic Checklists: Get task lists that adapt based on order type, customer priority, or shipment urgency
  • Next Best Step: AI suggests the most efficient next action based on current workload and deadlines
  • Bottleneck Alerts: Get notified when workflows slow down with suggestions to resolve
  • Progress Tracking: See real-time status of multi-step processes like returns or quality holds
  • Your Autonomy: Guidance is optional—ignore suggestions and work your way anytime

Quality Gates

  • Pre-Execution Review: See exactly what will happen before any automation runs
  • Batch Approval: Review and approve multiple similar actions at once
  • Exception Flags: Unusual cases automatically flagged for manual review
  • Rollback Capability: Undo automated changes if something unexpected happens
  • Audit Trails: Complete history of who approved what and when for compliance

Your Control Points

Task & Workflow keeps you in command at every step

What You Control

  • Approve each proposed automation
  • Set approval frequency (every time, daily, weekly)
