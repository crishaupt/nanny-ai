How Nanny-AI Mentors Your Work

Proactive guidance, contextual coaching, and skill development across your everyday tasks

Communication

Draft emails with selectable tone options, summarize long threads with citations, and rewrite documents for different audiences—all as editable suggestions you approve.

  • Draft messages with tone control
  • Summarize threads with references
  • Rewrite for different audiences
  • Preview before sending

Meetings & Scheduling

Propose agendas from recent documents, capture live notes and decisions, then offer post-meeting reviews before distribution—nothing happens automatically.

  • Agenda proposals from context
  • Live note capture with review
  • Follow-up draft generation
  • Calendar suggestions (not auto-booking)

Research & Knowledge

Retrieve and synthesize information into cited briefs, generate comparative overviews with explicit trade-offs, and link back to evidence for verification.

  • Briefs with cited sources
  • Alternative summaries for selection
  • Comparative analysis with trade-offs
  • Links to source material

Task & Workflow

Detect repetitive steps and propose lightweight automations with human-in-the-loop prompts. Suggest CRM updates you can accept, edit, or discard in one place.

  • CRM/ticket update suggestions

  • Request missing inputs before execution

Data & Analysis

Get first-pass analysis with visible assumptions and dataset references. Recommend formulas with plain-language explanations and preview effects before applying.

  • Analysis with visible assumptions
  • Dataset references included

  • Preview before apply

Learning & Coaching
