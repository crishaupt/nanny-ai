Research Capabilities for Supply Chain & Distribution

Everything you need to make informed logistics and sourcing decisions

Cited Briefs

  • Supplier Research: Compare capabilities, certifications, lead times, and pricing with visible sources
  • Market Intelligence: Synthesize freight rate trends, capacity forecasts, and fuel surcharge patterns
  • Technology Evaluation: Analyze WMS, TMS, or visibility platform options with feature comparisons
  • Compliance Updates: Summarize regulatory changes affecting cross-border shipping or hazmat transport
  • Citation Links: Every statement links back to source document for verification

Comparative Analysis

  • Carrier Selection: Compare cost, service levels, geographic coverage, and technology capabilities
  • Warehouse Location: Analyze labor costs, proximity to customers, tax incentives, and lease rates
  • Packaging Options: Evaluate materials, sustainability, cost per unit, and damage protection
  • Route Optimization: Compare direct ship vs. hub-and-spoke models with cost-service trade-offs
  • Explicit Trade-Offs: Clearly show advantages and disadvantages of each option

Multiple Perspectives

  • Executive Overview: 1-page summary with key findings and recommendations
  • Detailed Analysis: Comprehensive 5-10 page deep dive with all data points
  • Financial Focus: Cost-centric view highlighting total landed cost implications
  • Service Focus: Performance-centric view emphasizing speed, reliability, and customer impact
  • You Choose: Select the perspective that matches your current decision needs

Transparent Assumptions

  • Data Freshness: Clearly show when each data source was last updated
  • Gap Identification: Highlight missing information that could affect decisions
  • Assumption Documentation: List all assumptions made (e.g., "assuming 5% annual volume growth")
  • Confidence Levels: Indicate which conclusions are well-supported vs. preliminary
  • Recommended Next Steps: Suggest additional research needed for high-stakes decisions
