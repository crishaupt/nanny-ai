Meeting Capabilities for Supply Chain & Distribution

Everything you need to coordinate effective logistics and operations meetings

Agenda Proposals

  • Context-Aware: Suggest topics based on recent shipment delays, inventory alerts, and pending decisions
  • Priority Ranking: Order agenda items by urgency and business impact
  • Relevant Data: Attach shipment reports, carrier performance metrics, or inventory snapshots
  • Time Allocation: Propose realistic time slots for each discussion topic
  • You Approve: Edit, add, remove, or reorder items before sending invites

Live Note Capture

  • Decision Tracking: Automatically identify and highlight key decisions made during discussion
  • Action Items: Capture who's responsible, what needs done, and when it's due
  • Follow-Up Items: Note topics that need future meetings or additional research
  • Key Points: Summarize important discussion points without recording word-for-word
  • Post-Meeting Review: Edit and refine notes before distribution

Follow-Up Drafts

  • Task Creation: Convert action items into project management tasks with one click
  • Owner Notifications: Draft individual follow-up emails to action item owners
  • Deadline Reminders: Set up automatic reminders for approaching due dates
  • Progress Tracking: Prepare next meeting agenda based on completed/pending items
  • Approval Required: Review all follow-ups before they're created or sent

Smart Scheduling

  • Calendar Analysis: Find times when all required attendees are available
  • Timezone Coordination: Account for warehouse locations across different time zones
  • Recurring Patterns: Suggest consistent weekly/monthly slots for operations reviews
  • Buffer Time: Automatically include prep time before and wrap-up time after
  • You Choose: Select from suggested times or manually pick your preferred slot
