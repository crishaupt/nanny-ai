Our Core Principles

Four foundational principles guide every feature and interaction

1. Human in Control

Accountability and intent stay with you through approvals, undo, and "appropriate friction" at key moments.

  • Approval gates: High-impact actions like send/share require explicit permission
  • Undo available: Change your mind anytime with easy rollback paths
  • Appropriate friction: Critical moments pause for your review and decision
  • You decide: Every final action requires your explicit approval

2. Orchestrate, Don't Replace

AI coordinates tasks, people, and information seamlessly behind the scenes while you focus on judgment, creativity, and strategic decisions.

  • Automatic coordination: AI connects dots across your work without manual effort
  • Human decisions: You apply judgment and creativity on important matters
  • Simple interface: Complex coordination through easy-to-understand controls
  • Strategic focus: Spend time on high-value work that requires human insight and leadership

3. Transparent & Explainable

See sources, context, and limitations to enable informed edits and acceptance of AI outputs.

  • Visible sources: Every output shows where information came from
  • Clear assumptions: Understand what the AI assumed to generate results
  • Model limitations: Know what the AI can't do or doesn't know
  • Plain language: Explanations you can understand without technical expertise

4. Responsible by Design

Built-in guardrails aligned to privacy, fairness, and harm prevention commitments.

  • Privacy first: Clear policies about data use, retention, and protection
  • Safety policies: Guardrails prevent harmful or inappropriate outputs
  • Fairness commitment: Designed to reduce bias and promote equitable outcomes
  • Clear notices: Always informed about sensitive operations and data handling

What Nanny-AI Doesn't Automate

Some decisions require human judgment—and should stay that way

We believe certain decisions are fundamentally human. Nanny-AI intentionally avoids end-to-end automation in contexts where errors are costly, outcomes affect well-being, or decisions require empathy and ethical judgment.

High-Stakes Decisions

Situations where errors are costly or outcomes affect well-being, finances, or critical business operations.

  • Financial commitments and contracts
  • Personnel decisions (hiring, firing, promotion)
  • Legal or compliance matters
  • Safety-critical operations

Empathy & Negotiation

Decisions that hinge on empathy, negotiation, or ethical trade-offs where human judgment is essential.

  • Conflict resolution and mediation
  • Sensitive client or employee communications
  • Ethical dilemmas and trade-offs
  • Relationship management

Ambiguous Contexts

End-to-end execution in ambiguous or unclear situations where the right path isn't obvious.

  • Novel situations without clear precedent
  • Strategic pivots and major changes
  • Complex stakeholder management
  • Crisis response and recovery

Our Commitment

When situations require human judgment, Nanny-AI steps back and supports your decision-making with information and analysis—never replacing your responsibility to make the final call.

Why "Nanny-AI"?

The caring, protective philosophy behind our name

Like a trusted nanny, we provide care and protection—but we never replace the parent's role and responsibility. A good nanny proposes ideas, explains their reasoning, and helps nurture growth—but always defers to the parent for final decisions.

Similarly, Nanny-AI proposes assistance, previews outcomes, and explains rationale—but you remain firmly in control of your work, your decisions, and your business.

Protective

We guard against common pitfalls, mistakes, and costly errors through built-in safety measures.

Nurturing

We help you grow your capabilities through coaching, feedback, and skill development over time.
