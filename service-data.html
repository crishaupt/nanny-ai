Analysis Capabilities for Supply Chain & Distribution

Generate insights and reports while maintaining complete data transparency

Analysis Drafts

  • Carrier Performance: On-time delivery rates, cost per shipment, damage claims, and transit time trends
  • Inventory Analysis: Turnover ratios, stockout frequency, carrying costs, and slow-moving SKU identification
  • Warehouse Productivity: Units per hour, order accuracy, space utilization, and labor efficiency
  • Cost Breakdowns: Transportation spend by lane, storage costs by SKU, total landed cost analysis
  • Visible Assumptions: Every calculation shows what assumptions were made and why

Formula Recommendations

  • Plain Language Explanations: "This formula calculates cost per mile by dividing total freight spend by miles traveled"
  • Preview Effects: See results before applying formula changes to your reports
  • Scenario Modeling: Test "what if" scenarios with different assumptions (e.g., 10% volume increase impact)
  • Error Detection: AI flags formulas that produce unexpected results for your review
  • Rollback Capability: Undo formula changes if results aren't what you expected

Visualization Generation

  • Auto Chart Selection: AI recommends best chart type for your data (bar, line, pie, heatmap)
  • Trend Highlighting: Automatically emphasize significant changes or outliers in visual form
  • Comparison Views: Side-by-side carrier performance, warehouse efficiency, or period-over-period trends
  • Editable Graphics: Modify colors, labels, scales, and data ranges before finalizing
  • Export Options: Download as PDF, PowerPoint, or Excel with all source data included

Transparent Assumptions

  • Data Source Documentation: Every number traces back to specific database queries or file imports
  • Calculation Methods: Shows formulas used, rounding rules applied, and statistical methods employed
  • Time Period Clarity: Explicitly states date ranges, fiscal periods, and any excluded timeframes
  • Limitation Disclosure: Highlights incomplete data, missing information, or low confidence areas
  • Assumption Editing: Change any assumption and see updated analysis immediately
