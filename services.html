Communication Coaching

Get guided support for drafting, summarizing, and tailoring communications—with coaching on tone, clarity, and effectiveness.

Draft Messages

  • Selectable tone options (professional, friendly, formal)
  • Context-aware suggestions from recent conversations
  • Editable preview before any send action
  • Never auto-sends—you always click "Send"

Summarize Threads

  • Condense long email chains into key points
  • Inline citations for quick verification
  • Propose concise responses you can edit
  • References to original messages included

Rewrite & Tailor

Adapt documents for different audiences with piece-by-piece acceptance. Accept, edit, or reject each suggestion individually—never auto-applied.

Meetings & Scheduling

Propose agendas, capture decisions, and prepare follow-ups—with review gates before distribution.

Agenda Proposals

  • Generate agendas from recent documents and chats
  • Suggest calendar slots for your confirmation
  • No automatic booking—you choose when to schedule
  • Edit and refine before sending invites

Live Note Capture

  • Capture decisions, action items, and key points
  • Post-meeting review to confirm accuracy
  • Edit before distribution to attendees
  • Nothing shared until you approve

Follow-Up Drafts

Prepare follow-up messages and task assignments with one-tap approval. Review and edit before anything is created or sent to team members.

Research & Knowledge

Retrieve and synthesize information with cited sources and explicit trade-offs for informed decisions.

Cited Briefs

  • Short briefs with visible source citations
  • Multiple alternative summaries for selection
  • Links back to evidence for verification
  • Transparent about data limitations

Comparative Analysis

  • Generate overviews with explicit trade-offs
  • Make advantages and disadvantages clear
  • Reference sources for deeper inspection
  • Highlight data gaps and assumptions

Multiple Perspectives

Choose from different summary styles and depths. Select executive overview, detailed analysis, or key insights only—tailored to your immediate need.

CRM & System Updates

  • Suggest field changes you can review
  • Accept, edit, or discard in one place
  • No auto-commits to databases

Data & Analysis

Analysis Drafts

  • Generate charts and analysis with visible assumptions
  • Include dataset references for verification
  • Verify before sharing with stakeholders
  • Edit narrative and visualizations freely
