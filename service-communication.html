Communication Capabilities for Supply Chain & Distribution

Everything you need to coordinate supplier, carrier, and customer communications

Draft Messages

  • Supplier Updates: Draft purchase order confirmations, specification changes, and quality issue notifications
  • Carrier Coordination: Generate shipping instructions, pickup requests, and route change communications
  • Customer Notifications: Create delivery updates, delay explanations, and shipment confirmations
  • Tone Control: Choose professional, friendly, urgent, or apologetic tone for any message
  • Never Auto-Sends: You always review and click "Send"

Summarize Threads

  • Long Email Chains: Condense multi-day supplier negotiations into key points and decisions
  • Carrier Communications: Extract critical shipping updates from lengthy carrier threads
  • Customer Inquiries: Summarize repeat customer questions across multiple channels
  • Inline Citations: Click any summary point to see the original message
  • Quick Verification: References to source emails included for accuracy checking

Rewrite & Tailor

  • Multi-Audience Adaptation: Take one message and tailor it for suppliers, carriers, and customers
  • Formality Adjustment: Convert casual internal notes to professional external communications
  • Language Simplification: Translate technical shipping terms into plain language for customers
  • Piece-by-Piece Control: Accept, edit, or reject each suggestion individually
  • Never Auto-Applied: Every change requires your approval

Multi-Channel Coordination

  • Unified Context: Connect communications across email, SMS, portal messages, and calls
  • Channel-Appropriate Formatting: Auto-adapt message length and style for SMS vs email
  • Consistent Messaging: Ensure same information shared across all customer touchpoints
  • Context Preservation: Link related messages so you see full conversation history
  • One-Click Switching: Move draft from email to SMS with automatic reformatting
